# Project Naming Proposal

A working document to find the right name for this project.

## Context

This project brings the aesthetic, workflow, and application choices of
[OMARCHY](https://omarchy.com) to the COSMIC Desktop environment. We appreciate
the keyboard-first philosophy, the curated application selection (btop, ghostty,
lazyvim), and the polished look and feel.

What we're leaving behind: the author's editorial opinions that come bundled
with the distro. We're here for the workflow, not the worldview.

**Think of it as:** "I'll take the rice, hold the sermon."

## Naming Criteria

Based on your preferences:

- **Tone**: Cheeky rebellion - openly "defecting" with humor
- **COSMIC reference**: Yes, include it in the name
- **Themes**: Space/cosmic, order vs chaos (anarchy), remix/fork concepts

---

## Top Candidates

### 1. COSMIC ORDER

**Tagline**: *"Establishing order in the chaos"*

Direct antithesis to "anarchy." Clean, memorable, slightly authoritarian in a
tongue-in-cheek way. Implies structure and discipline (keyboard-first workflow)
without the accompanying ideology.

- `cosmic-order` (repo name)
- Plays on "New World Order" conspiracy vibes (ironic given the source)
- Logo potential: Cosmic spiral with geometric order

**Pros**: Clean, obvious contrast, professional enough for serious use
**Cons**: Might be too subtle for some

---

### 2. COSMARCHY

**Tagline**: *"OMARCHY, but make it COSMIC"*

Direct portmanteau. Doesn't hide the inspiration - wears it openly. Could be
read as "Cosmic Monarchy" (order!) or just "COSMIC + OMARCHY."

- `cosmarchy` (repo name)
- Clear lineage, no pretense
- Easy to explain: "It's OMARCHY for COSMIC Desktop"

**Pros**: Immediately clear what it is, memorable
**Cons**: Might be seen as too derivative

---

### 3. THE COSMIC WAY

**Tagline**: *"A better path through the stars"*

Direct parody of "The Omarchy Manual." Implies there's a "correct" way to do
things - our way. Has a philosophical/spiritual tone that gently mocks the
sometimes preachy nature of the source material.

- `cosmic-way` (repo name)
- Documentation: "The Cosmic Way Manual"
- Pairs well with "This is the way" meme energy

**Pros**: Great documentation tie-in, memorable phrase
**Cons**: Might sound too new-age

---

### 4. COSMIC REMIX

**Tagline**: *"Same great taste, different opinions"*

Acknowledges we're remixing someone else's work. Honest about the relationship.
Music industry term that implies improvement/reinterpretation.

- `cosmic-remix` (repo name)
- Could version as "Cosmic Remix 2024" etc.
- Logo: Waveform or mixing board with cosmic theme

**Pros**: Honest, modern, implies creative improvement
**Cons**: "Remix" might undersell the technical work

---

### 5. COSMIC PROTOCOL

**Tagline**: *"Structured rebellion"*

"Protocol" implies rules, structure, and keyboard-first discipline. Contrasts
nicely with "anarchy." Has a technical, no-nonsense feel while still being
a bit cheeky (protocols for rebelling against the source material).

- `cosmic-protocol` (repo name)
- Documentation: "Protocol Specifications"
- Fits the keyboard-first, CLI workflow vibe

**Pros**: Technical feel, implies discipline and structure
**Cons**: Might sound too enterprise-y

---

### 6. COSMIC DEFECTOR

**Tagline**: *"We liked the workflow, not the manifesto"*

Openly positions this as a defection from OMARCHY. Honest, slightly
confrontational, but with humor. A "defector" takes the good stuff and leaves.

- `cosmic-defector` (repo name)
- Implies bringing "secrets" (the good config) to the other side
- Cold War vibes in a fun way

**Pros**: Very clear positioning, memorable, conversation starter
**Cons**: Might be too aggressive for some

---

### 7. FORKED COSMOS

**Tagline**: *"Fork the opinions, keep the aesthetic"*

Git terminology meets cosmic theme. Every developer knows what a fork means -
taking something and going your own direction. "Forked" also works as mild
profanity substitute for extra cheek.

- `forked-cosmos` (repo name)
- Plays on "forked up" without being explicit
- Clear technical meaning

**Pros**: Developer humor, clear meaning, slightly edgy
**Cons**: Might be too cute

## Recommendation

My top pick: **COSMIC ORDER** or **COSMARCHY**

- **COSMIC ORDER** if you want something that stands more independently while
  still being a clear contrast to "anarchy"
- **COSMARCHY** if you want to openly acknowledge the lineage and don't mind
  being obviously derivative (in a good way)

Both work well as repo names, are memorable, and strike the right tone of
"cheeky rebellion" without being mean-spirited.

## Documentation Tone

Whichever name you choose, the documentation can maintain a consistent voice:

**Acknowledgment section example:**

> COSMIC ORDER draws heavy inspiration from [OMARCHY](https://omarchy.com),
> created by DHH. We admire the curated workflow, application choices, and
> keyboard-first philosophy. We're less enthusiastic about the bundled opinions.
>
> This project brings the aesthetic to COSMIC Desktop, letting you enjoy the
> rice without subscribing to the newsletter.

**Manual parody potential:**

Instead of "The Omarchy Manual," we could have:
- "The Cosmic Order Manual" - dry, official sounding
- "The Way of Cosmic Order" - slightly mystical parody
- "Protocol Documentation" - technical and straight-faced
